## Recap
[[M.I.D.A - Ruby]] and [[Urist - Adam]] spoke about the prior nights events, and [[Ash - Elliot]] spoke with [[Marra]], who said they would keep an eye out for any suspicious transactions within the guild hall

The party met up at the [[Dragonclaw Tavern]], where it was decided the [[Urist - Adam]] and [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]] would go to the Blacksmiths and [[Ash - Elliot]] decided to spend the morning working at the [[Eldermount's Alchemist Guild]].

[[Urist - Adam]] and [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]] went to the Blacksmith and met [[Cael Varen]], the cities renowned Blacksmith. [[Urist - Adam]] and [[Cael Varen]] competed in a smithing competition, which [[Cael Varen]] won, but he gave them the information they were looking for; [[Cael Varen]] made mention that they recognized [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]], and then when he got a delivery to deliver Pheonix pommels to a location in [[The Cinderways]]; which he gives [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]] and [[Urist - Adam]]

[[Ash - Elliot]] worked in the [[Eldermount's Alchemist Guild]] for the day, seeing [[Uriel Stormwind]] come in and buy some ingredients, and mention they were getting back into a hobby. A few hours later, when being abit sneaky, [[Ash - Elliot]] looked in the Guildmaster office aswell as papers being carried, discovering deals between the [[Eldermount's Alchemist Guild]] and [[House Malakai]], aswell as a new order from the [[The Cinderways]], worth 30-40g, and a delivery to the [[Ancient Ruins]], looking for Dragon's Blood as a Stabilizing Agent for *something*.

Later on, [[Ash - Elliot]] decides to look into an individual called [[Victor]], an unregistered alchemist who they had not heard from in awhile; the Address is the same address [[Cael Varen]] gave [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]] and [[Urist - Adam]] to deliver, so the party re-convenes in [[Eldermount#The Lower Tunnels|The Lower Tunnels]], and make their way to [[Victor]]'s address. When they arrive at the address, it appears to be ransacked, and upon further investigation, it is clear that he was kidnapped.
Whilst looking around, [[Ash - Elliot]] experiences an episode of PTSD, experiencing a dark memory of Project Pheonix

![[Ash - Elliot#Turning Point]]

[[Ash - Elliot]] and [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]] argued about the situation as [[Urist - Adam]] heard footsteps below, followed by glass smashing as the house is set ablaze. [[Urist - Adam]] drags the two out the back window, and the party run down the stone tunnels behind the house
