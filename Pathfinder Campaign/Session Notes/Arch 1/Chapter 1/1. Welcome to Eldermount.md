## Act 1 - The Festival
**Atmosphere**
- **Sights:**  People walking around, Stalls setup everywhere, Guards traversing around, the sound of food and drink being served
- **Sounds:**  People talking, Music playing
- **Smells:**  Food and drinks
-
**Scene Summary:**  
The party have each arrived to the [[Market Centre]] in the city of [[Eldermount]], whether they live in the city or have travelled from where they stay, to explore the city and participate in The Festival of Renewal

**GM Notes:**  
Encourage roleplay between players and NPCs, allow the players to get a feel for the characters they are playing

**Opportunities**
- **NPCs to Interact With:**
  - [[Corin Vale]] - Pawning off goods taken from some corrupt official
  - [[Taren Dusk]] - Speaking in prophecies; *"The Mountain Remembers"*
  - [[Captain Jorek Valeen]] - Watching the market, making sure nothing illegal happens
  - [[Magister Selera Vayne]] - Wondering around, looking at some magical items and grimoires

**Events:**
- [[Taren Dusk]] speaking in riddles, potentially mad, 
	- DC 15 Occultism Check (or 10 Lore: Curse): 
		- **Success:** He is not a madman, he is seeing some kind of vision, or something is showing him something
		- **Failure:** He is a madman, he is crazy
	- DC 10 Medicine Check:
		- **Success:** He is smoking, his eyes are glazed over, something is wrong
		- **Failure:** Nothing
- [[Captain Jorek Valeen]] is looking for any criminals, If PC has met and interacted with [[Corin Vale]], and he has revealed his latest escapade, they may assist either NPC
- Any magic used may catch the eye of [[Magister Selera Vayne]]
