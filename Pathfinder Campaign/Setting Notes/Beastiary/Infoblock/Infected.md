# Infected
**Creature Type:** Humanoid  
**Rarity:** Unique  
**Alignment:** Neutral  
**Source:** Homebrew  

---
## Overview
The Infected are the living remnants of failure; victims of [[Project Pheonix]] who survived its alchemical experiments, but not unchanged. Twisted by unstable compounds and volatile transformations, they exist somewhere between subject and byproduct, their bodies reshaped into unpredictable, often monstrous forms. Though called “Infected” by those who created them, the term is less a diagnosis and more a dismissal, reducing what were once people into something easier to contain, hunt, or forget.

## Appearance
At first glance, many of the Infected still resemble who they once were, their forms carrying enough familiarity to unsettle rather than reassure. Clothing hangs from warped frames, weapons are still clutched in trembling hands, and fragments of identity linger in posture or movement. But that illusion quickly fractures. Their bodies bear the mark of violent transformation—flesh stretched unevenly, limbs distorted or misaligned, and skin split by growths that resemble scales, chitin, or crystallized residue. Veins darken or glow faintly beneath the surface, pulsing with erratic life, while joints bend in ways that suggest their structure is no longer entirely human.

As the transformation progresses, coherence gives way to instability. Some develop exaggerated mass in certain areas—arms thickened into crude weapons or torsos swollen beyond proportion—while others seem to collapse inward, becoming gaunt, brittle, and twitching. Faces rarely remain intact; features warp, eyes cloud or multiply, and mouths split or elongate into something barely capable of speech. Many exhibit signs of internal pressure or leakage, venting heat, mist, or alchemical residue through cracks in their skin. Despite their monstrous forms, there are fleeting moments where something human surfaces—a hesitation, a glance, a sound that almost resembles a word—before the instability consumes it once again.

## Lore & Hooks
- The Infected are the discarded results of [[Project Pheonix]]—failed attempts to push mortal bodies beyond their limits using unstable alchemical infusions.
- While the goal of the project remains unclear to outsiders, its outcomes are not: transformation, instability, and loss of control.
- Not all “failures” are immediate. Some victims appear normal for days… before the changes begin.
