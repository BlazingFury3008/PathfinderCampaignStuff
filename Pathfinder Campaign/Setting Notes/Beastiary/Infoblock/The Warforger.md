# The Warforger
**Creature Type:** Aberration  
**Rarity:** Unique  
**Alignment:** Chaotic Evil  
**Source:** Homebrew  

---
## Overview
The Warforger dwells within a region of [[Ibbirus]] known as [[The World Anvil]]. Here, he endlessly forges weapons of unimaginable destruction. Though rarely seen, his influence reaches far beyond this place—haunting the dreams of creators and artisans alike, seeding their minds with flawless blueprints for things that should never be made. 

---
## Appearance
#### Manifested Form
The Warforger manifests as a towering mass of metal and fire, his form unstable, as though it was never meant to hold together. Machinery protrudes from him in broken layers—gears turning without purpose, mechanisms grinding long past the point of failure. Flames leak from within, not as fury, but as something contained too long.
His eyes burn with the intensity of [[The World Anvil]], steady and unblinking—like a fire that cannot be extinguished. There is no rage in him. No intent to destroy. Only the quiet, terrible presence of something that has already been made. and cannot be undone.
#### Subtle Form
He rarely needs to manifest. Instead, he speaks in dreams—quiet, patient, and impossible to resist. To artisans and inventors, his voice feels like revelation. Designs unfold in their minds with perfect clarity, as though they were always meant to be discovered.
They do not question it; until it is finished. The realization does not come from the voice, but from the thing itself. In the completed work, they see its purpose, its potential… and its cost. By then, it is too late. The knowledge cannot be unlearned, nor the creation undone. What they once believed to be inspiration becomes a burden they must carry, knowing they were the ones who gave it form.
