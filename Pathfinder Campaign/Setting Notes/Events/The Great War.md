## Overview
The Great War was a global threat, It saw the world of Ammiryon brought to the brink of destructions, all the continents and many subfactions came together to fight the forces invading from [[Ibbirus]]

---
## Causes
What led to this event?
- A group known as [[The Arcanum]] were performing magical experiments, which incidentally caused a rift between this realm and [[Ibbirus]] to open; and the Armies from [[Ibbirus]] noticed, and decided to invade

---
## Timeline/Description
The Great War was the largest scale conflict the realm has ever seen. 
#### The Rift -1634
Whilst [[The Arcanum]] were performing their magical experiments into higher planer entities caused barriers between planes to weaken, as a result, demonic entities from the plane of [[Ibbirus]] were able to break through and begin to wreak havoc and chaos apon the land

#### The Summit - 1635
A year after The war began, leaders of nations and factions all came together to form a coalition against the rampaging demons.
