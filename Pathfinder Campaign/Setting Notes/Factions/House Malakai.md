## Overview
House Malakai is one of the most powerful and far-reaching arcane institutions in the realm. Widely regarded as the largest collective of magic users in existence, the House operates independently of any single kingdom or empire, instead answering to its own leadership and principles
Publically, House Malakai is known for advancing magical research and advising rulers on arcane matters; Their Chantries are fortified centres of learning and containment, and are found all over the continents, serving as hubs of scholarship, training and regulation. Whilst many kingdoms rely on their advisors in times of magical crisis, very few actually trust their long-term intentions
