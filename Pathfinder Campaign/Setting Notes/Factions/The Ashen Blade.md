## Overview
The Ashen Blade is both the name of a group and of their ship. They are one of Eldermount’s worst kept secrets, a loosely organised band of thieves, smugglers and saboteurs who operated on the edge of the law, and often directly against it. To the city’s authorities, they are criminals; however to the commonfolk, they are folk heroes
The Ashen Blade are known for targeting the wealthy, the corrupt and the powerful rather than common citizens, Their exploits are spoken in taverns all over the city: vaults emptied overnight, caravans ‘redistributed’ and corrupt officials exposed or embarrassed without a drop of blood spilled.
