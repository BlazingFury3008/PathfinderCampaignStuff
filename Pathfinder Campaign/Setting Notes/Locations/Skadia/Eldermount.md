## Description
Eldermount is the capital city of the continent of Skadia. It is built around the top of a long-domant volcano. Rather than sitting atop the mountain, the city is carved into its stone, with streets, districts, and foundations woven directly into the volcanic rock. Magma that still flows deep below is harnessed to heat forges and workshops, making Eldermount a renowned center of smithing and craftsmanship within the continent. Beneath the city, natural caverns and ancient tunnels form an unseen underworld that influences both daily life and local legend

---
### Districts
#### The Upper Rings
The Upper Rings sit at the highest elevation of the city, built surrounding the mouth of the dormant volcano. This district is the centre of scholarship, magical study and governance within the city. Libraries, Academies and power institutions dominate the skyline. It is difficult to get access to the Upper Rings, requiring invitation from those who reside there; Typically being those who show magical potential or archmages from other cities

#### The Mid City
The Mid City is the hub of Eldermount, comprising the majority of the city's population and daily activities. In the centre of the city lies the [[Market Centre]], surrounded by a sprawl of houses, guild halls, workshops. The streets are lined with rune-lanterns, and the city is built with great care and skilled labor

#### The Lower Tunnels
Beneath the main city lie the Lower Tunnels, a vast network of natural chambers. Official maps mark the tunnels as restricted no-access zones, however in reality they hold the cities second largest market. It is an area unspoken unless you are already inside
