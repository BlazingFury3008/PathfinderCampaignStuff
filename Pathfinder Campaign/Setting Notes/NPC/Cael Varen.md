## Appearance
At first glance, Cael Varen appears to be an unremarkable human: quiet, composed, and perpetually marked by the soot and heat of the forge. Their build is notably lean, almost wiry—an oddity for someone who spends each day shaping metal beneath hammer and flame. When at work, their movements are precise and controlled, each action deliberate, as though nothing is ever wasted, not even effort. There is a stillness to them that feels out of place, as though their body moves, but something within remains distant and unmoving.
Upon closer inspection, subtle details begin to surface. Their skin, though often dulled by ash and labour, carries a faint, ashen pallor beneath the grime—like something long removed from true warmth. Their eyes reflect light too sharply, not with brightness, but with a muted, glasslike sheen, as if the world reaches them from a slight remove. At times, in the right light, faint markings can be seen beneath their skin—thin, vein-like traces that resemble cooled fractures in stone or old, sealed scars, hinting at something that has been broken and made whole again.

Cael rarely reveals the truth of what they are. When they do, it manifests not as something radiant, but as something quiet and final. A single black wing unfurls from their back, its form imperfect and heavy, more reminiscent of shadow than feather. At the same time, dim, gold-tinged runes etch themselves across their arms and collarbone—not glowing with life, but with a subdued, funerary light, like embers long past their flame. The air around them grows still in those moments, carrying the faint, unsettling sense of something that has already crossed the boundary of death, and returned unchanged.

## Background
Cael is old, older than most mortal records can remember, their existence stretching back hundreds if not thousands of years, back to the time of [[The Great War]]. During this war, they were not a soldier, nor bound to any banner, but instead a prisoner held within [[The World Anvil]] by [[The Warforger]]. Within [[The World Anvil]], Cael was forced to forge weapons to help the Invasion.

Once [[The Great War]] came to an end, Cael found themselves wondering, along their travels they came across [[M.I.D.A - Ruby]], however at this moment they were not sentient, instead they seemed to be deactivated.
