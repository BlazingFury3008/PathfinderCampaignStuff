## Overview
Unexplored and presumed dangerous tunnels deep in [[Eldermount#The Lower Tunnels|The Lower Tunnels of Eldermount]]


---
## Description
“The Depths” is not a singular location, but instead a name given to the deeper unexplored regions beyond The Lower Tunnels. Exiting The Lower Tunnels towards the depths is prohibited, and a guard is almost always standing watch. Very few have gone into “The Depths” and even less have returned
