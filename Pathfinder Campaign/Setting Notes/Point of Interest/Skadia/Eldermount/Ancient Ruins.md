## Overview

---
## Description
In the deeper sections of the Lower Tunnels exist large stone structures. Cordoned off by researchers, these are oversized arches, immense stairways and worn carvings that suggest the ruins of a settlement that predates the city. Common belief holds that these are the remains a city of giants, although no official record confirms who lived there or why it fell
