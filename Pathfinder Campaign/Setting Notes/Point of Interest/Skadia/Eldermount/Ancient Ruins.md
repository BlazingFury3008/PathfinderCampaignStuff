## Overview
Ancients ruins within the [[Eldermount#The Lower Tunnels|The Lower Tunnels of Eldermount]]

---
## Description
In the deeper sections of the Lower Tunnels exist large stone structures. Cordoned off by researchers, these are oversized arches, immense stairways and worn carvings that suggest the ruins of a settlement that predates the city. Common belief holds that these are the remains a city of giants, although no official record confirms who lived there or why it fell

---
## History / Lore

The [[Ancient Ruins]] beneath [[Eldermount]] are not a single structure, but a fragmented expanse of buried architecture, unearthed only in part. What has been uncovered suggests that these ruins were not built for mortals, nor even for giants as they are understood today, but for something far greater.
### Excavated

The Excavated Area is the only portion of the ruins made accessible to the public and sanctioned researchers. Over decades, scholars and laborers have carefully unearthed sections of immense stonework buried beneath the mountain.
What has been revealed are vast, monolithic structures—pillars, archways, and chambers of impossible scale—crafted from stone that does not match any known quarry within [[Skadia]]. These structures are believed to be remnants of ancient creatures, or perhaps beings that once shaped the world itself.
Despite ongoing study, the purpose of these constructions remains unknown. Many within [[House Malakai]] theorise that the ruins predate recorded history entirely, existing long before the rise of mortal civilization.

#### Uncovered
Beyond the known excavation sites, the ruins extend far deeper. Recent tremors within [[Eldermount#The Lower Tunnels|The Lower Tunnels]] have fractured previously sealed passageways. One of these such breaks lies behind [[Victor]]'s residence, where a newly opened tunnel descends into an uncharted section of the ruins

#### Tunnels Behind Victor's
If the party follow this path, they will emerge into a vast chamber unlike anything found in [[Ancient Ruins#Excavated|The Excavated Area]]. At first glance, it appears to be a normal stone chamber, however upon further inspection, it appears to be a throne room, but not one for any creature they can comprehend. The size and scale is overwhelming, the steps leading upwards are the size of buildings. The throne itself is not merely large, its colossal, seemingly carved from a single slab of unnaturally dark stone.
The chamber itself does not feel abandoned. Instead, it feels like it is waiting. The architecture here is more refined than the sections in [[Ancient Ruins#Excavated|The Excavated Area]], as though this place was sealed by intention instead of time.
As the party looks around, they will notice that at the base of the throne appears to be the remnants of a campsite, very few supplies left, but there is a book left, a journal; It will take some time to decipher, but it will eventually be deciphered to give a name, [[Dr Frederick Gransley]]
