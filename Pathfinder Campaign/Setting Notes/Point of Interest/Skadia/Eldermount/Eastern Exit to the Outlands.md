## Description
To the south east of the city lies the Eastern Exit gate. This is the pathway most commonly used by merchants, caravans and any official traffic travelling towards Eastern Skadia. Although seemingly narrow at first, the further way from the city you get, the wider the path becomes; The pathway is guarded and regularly maintained
