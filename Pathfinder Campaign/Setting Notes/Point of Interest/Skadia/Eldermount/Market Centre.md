## Description
The Market Centre is the heart of Eldermount, and the busiest location in the city. It is a wide open plaza surrounded by layered stalls, permanent shops and guild outposts. Almost anything can be bought from here, as merchants from all over the realm pass through, so two different days may lead to entirely different merchandise. The Market Centre is a melting pot of cultures, rumours and opportunities. During the Festival of Renewal, it is a focal point of celebration, filled with banners, musics and crows at all hours
