## Description
The most infamous region of the Lower Tunnels is the Cinderways; Known as Eldermount’s Black Market. It is built across intersecting caverns and lava channels, The Cinderways operates in a state of a necessary evil. Here, forbidden goods, rare artifacts and dangerous information changes hands. Whereas markets take gold, the Cinderways is built on trade. You want information from someone, you better have something to trade other than coin. Most pretend the Cinderways do not exist, Most also know exactly where to find it
