## Description
To the west of the city lies the mountain, forming both a natural barrier as well as a constant reminder of the city's precarious location. From the mountain flows a constant river of magma, channeled to heat the forges that reside along the west wall of the city.

### Underground
Beneath the mountain, the tunnels give way to a vast, open cavern where a churning pool of magma casts a dim, hellish glow across jagged stone. The air is stifling, thick with heat and the scent of burning earth, while narrow, crumbling paths wind precariously along the cavern’s edge. Only a handful of natural tunnels connect to this space, each unstable and treacherous, where a single misstep could mean a fall into the molten depths below.
