## Description
The most prominent structure within the Upper Rings is the Chantry of House Malakai, also known as the Obsidian Vault. Seemingly built into the mouth of the volcano, its external building is etched in wards, sigils and other magical frameworks that glow faintly even at rest. Whilst the Chantry is presented as a place of scholarship and research, many believe it has large political influence, shaping many policies that affect the use of magic within the city
