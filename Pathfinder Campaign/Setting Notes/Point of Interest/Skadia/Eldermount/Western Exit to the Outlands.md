---
## Description
The Western exit is older, narrower and less maintained than the Eastern Exit. Cutting through [[The Mountains]], the route is often effected by tremors that plague the city due to its location. Despite how dangerous and unpredictable the route may be, it still remains vital, especially for less regulated traffic and those that prefer speed or discretion over safety
